Primary Assessment - (ABC)

Airway Clear?

Head Tilt and Chin Lift - (Clear tongue from airway)

Patient Breathing?

Look, listen, and feel. Is chest rising?

Circulation

Pulse and Bleeding. If heavy bleeding, address it.

Is Breathing, Has Pulse
  • Treat for shock
  • Monitor Vitals

Not Breathing, Has Pulse
  • Head tilt, chin lift
  • Rescue Breathing
  • One breath every 5 seconds or one every three seconds for child or infant
  • If air goes in continue rescue breathing and check for pulse and breathing every two minutes
  • If air does not go in - Airway Obstruction.
  • Do 30 chest compressions, open mouth and look in. Use finger sweep to remove any dislodged stuff. Do two rescue breaths.
  • Repeat chest compressions and rescue breaths until airway clears.
  • Check pulse and breathing when airway clears
  • If pulse stops go to CPR

Not Breathing, No Pulse
  • CPR
  • Call 911, get an AED
  • For adult, start with 30 chest compressions then head-tilt/chin-lift and two breaths
  • For child, start with two breaths then 30 chest compressions and continue cycle
  • If two people are available do 15 chest compressions and then two breaths
  • If an AED arrives have partner continue CPR and lead rescuer (me) sets up AED.
  • Turn on AED and follow directions from AED
